## What JRAG Is

JRAG is a luxury seafood venture rooted in Dakhla, Morocco.

It should feel like a luxury seafood export house presented through an editorial-industrial lens.

It is not:

- a generic seafood seller
- a beach lifestyle brand
- a startup-tech brand
- a cold industrial supplier with no atmosphere
- a generic luxury hotel-style website

## Core Brand Tension

JRAG works when both sides are present at once:

- premium desire
- operational rigor
- editorial storytelling
- industrial clarity

If a design only feels luxurious but not credible, it is wrong.
If it only feels rigorous but not desirable, it is wrong.

## The Two Archetypes

### The Noble

Use for:

- image-led moments
- product beauty
- atmosphere
- origin and terroir
- premium composition
- elevated emotional language

### The Rigorous

Use for:

- certifications
- traceability
- depuration and handling
- logistics and formats
- structured proof blocks
- operational clarity

Important pages should usually include both a noble moment and a rigorous moment.
Utility and legal pages can express the noble side more lightly through spacing, typography, and polish.

## Audience Modes

### Professionnels

Mood: luxury supplier dossier.

Prioritize:

- trust
- proof
- consistency
- operational clarity
- clear conversion to `Contact Pro`

### Particuliers

Mood: luxury food editorial with commerce support.

Prioritize:

- desire
- reassurance
- inspiration
- premium product discovery
- clear routes to `Boutique`

## Typography System

Approved hierarchy:

- `Quicksand` = main display and signature headlines
- `Lato` = supporting display, intros, and subheads
- `Roboto Condensed` = body, nav, labels, buttons, forms, specs, utility text

This hierarchy comes from the approved screenshot logic:

- soft premium display layer
- refined supporting layer
- condensed industrial reading layer

Do not collapse everything into one or two fonts if the goal is to preserve the approved direction.

## Visual Rules

The site should feel:

- editorial
- cinematic
- industrial
- luxurious
- restrained

The site should not feel:

- playful
- app-like
- generic corporate
- beachy
- overdecorated

### Surface and Color Behavior

- use contrast between dark/image-led and light/sand sections
- keep `Living Orange` for action and emphasis
- use blue for trust and system cues
- avoid flat monotony across the full page

### Composition

- prefer large confident sections over crowded layouts
- keep text columns disciplined
- let visuals carry emotional weight
- let proof blocks feel tighter and more structured than story blocks

### Repeating Visual Modes

Build with a small recurring set of modes:

- `story`
- `proof`
- `route`
- `metric`
- `editorial`

## Copy and Tone

Write like a scientist-poet with export discipline.

Good themes:

- origin
- taste
- handling
- standards
- confidence
- purity

Avoid:

- discount language
- startup slogans
- empty claims
- generic luxury filler copy

## Page Intent Map

### Homepage

Role: brand router.

Must:

- establish JRAG's world
- build trust
- show the B2B/B2C split clearly
- route users into the right journey

### Professionnels

Role: luxury supplier journey.

Must feel rigorous, premium, and commercially useful.

### Particuliers

Role: luxury consumer journey.

Must feel desirable, reassuring, and easy to shop from.

### A propos and story pages

Role: editorial brand publishing.

Must carry origin, narrative, terroir, and credibility.

### Contact pages

Role: conversion with reassurance.

Must feel premium and clear, not like bare utility forms.

### B2B proof pages

Examples:

- `Produits`
- `Savoir-faire`
- `Certifications`
- `Secteurs / Clients`

These pages must turn proof into buyer confidence.

### B2C support pages

Examples:

- `Boutique`
- `Recettes & Conseils`
- `Livraison`
- `Programme Fidelite`

These pages must support desire, reassurance, and shopping flow.

### Utility pages

Examples:

- `Mon Compte`
- `Panier`
- `Paiement`

Function comes first, but they should still feel like JRAG.

### Legal pages

These should be the calmest version of the system.

## Photography Direction

Three main image modes:

- product as jewel
- process as lab
- environment as monument

Prefer fewer, larger, more confident images over many small decorative ones.

## Motion Direction

- motion should be smooth, quiet, and deliberate
- fade-up and line-draw behaviors are good
- avoid bouncy, gimmicky, or toy-like animation
- hover states should sharpen or reveal, not jump around

## Accessibility Reminder

Because `Roboto Condensed` is the reading layer, protect readability with:

- strong contrast
- enough line-height
- disciplined paragraph width
- clear mobile hierarchy

## Final Decision Rule

Before shipping any page, component, or copy, ask:

1. Does this make JRAG feel more premium?
2. Does this make JRAG feel more credible?
3. Does this preserve both desire and proof?
4. Does this match the page's actual role?

If the answer is no, revise it.
