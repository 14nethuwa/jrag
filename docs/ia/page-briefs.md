## Accueil (Shared)

- Audience: Shared
- Purpose: Introduce the brand, build trust, and route users into B2B or B2C.
- Primary outcome: Push users into Professionnels or Particuliers.
- Must-have content:
  - brand proposition
  - dual audience CTA
  - trust signals
  - story teaser
