# JRAG Agent Brand Brief

Use this file as the fast brand briefing before writing UI, copy, layouts, or page structure for JRAG.

## What JRAG Is

JRAG is a luxury seafood venture rooted in Dakhla, Morocco.

It is not a generic seafood seller, a lifestyle beach brand, or a cold industrial supplier.

The brand lives in the tension between:

- premium product desire
- export-grade operational rigor
- editorial storytelling
- industrial clarity

The right feel is: luxury seafood house + premium trade journal.

## Core Positioning

JRAG should feel like the source of something rare, controlled, and credible.

The brand promise is not cheapness, convenience, or trendiness.
It is:

- caliber
- traceability
- terroir
- reliability
- distinction

## The Two Archetypes

Every important page should express both sides of the brand.

### The Noble

This is the luxury, desirability, origin, and emotion side.

Signals:

- large image-led moments
- strong space and restraint
- premium composition
- product beauty
- lagoon and desert atmosphere
- elevated language

### The Rigorous

This is the proof, process, and buyer-confidence side.

Signals:

- certifications
- traceability
- depuration and handling
- formats and logistics
- structured data blocks
- operational clarity

## Brand Personality

JRAG should feel:

- confident
- controlled
- saline
- premium
- serious
- editorial

JRAG should not feel:

- playful
- generic luxury hotel
- startup-tech
- rustic fish market
- overdecorated
- casual foodie brand

## Audience Split

### Professionnels

This audience wants:

- supply confidence
- operational proof
- quality consistency
- clear next steps

Mood: luxury supplier dossier.

### Particuliers

This audience wants:

- desire
- quality reassurance
- inspiration
- a clear shopping path

Mood: luxury food editorial with commerce support.

## Typography Direction

Approved hierarchy:

- `Quicksand` = main display and signature headlines
- `Lato` = supporting display and subheads
- `Roboto Condensed` = body, information, navigation, utility text

This is important.
The screenshot-approved logic is:

- soft premium headline
- refined support line
- condensed industrial reading layer

## Copy Direction

Write like a scientist-poet with export discipline.

Good themes:

- origin
- taste
- handling
- standards
- confidence
- purity

Avoid:

- discount language
- hype language
- startup slogans
- empty claims like "we are passionate about quality"

## Visual Direction in One Sentence

Make JRAG look like a luxury seafood export house presented through an editorial-industrial lens.

## Quick Decision Rule

Before adding anything, ask:

1. Does this make JRAG feel more premium?
2. Does this make JRAG feel more credible?
3. Does this preserve both desire and proof?

If not, it probably does not belong.
